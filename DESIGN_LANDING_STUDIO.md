## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

### Visual Design
- **Dark theme** (reduces eye strain for long writing sessions)
- **Minimal distractions** (focus on content)
- **Smooth transitions** between stages
- **Consistent iconography** across stages

### UX Principles
- **Progressive disclosure** (show only what's needed)
- **Keyboard shortcuts** for power users
- **Mobile-responsive** (view on tablets during production)
- **Offline capability** (work without internet, sync later)

### AI Integration
- **Non-intrusive** (suggestions, not interruptions)
- **Transparent** (explain AI reasoning)
- **Controllable** (user can accept/reject/modify)
- **Learnable** (improves with use)
